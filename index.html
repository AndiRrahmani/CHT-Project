Hotels

Aman New York (New York)

The Aman New York is one of the most luxurious hotels in Manhattan, located in the iconic Crown Building near Central Park. Opened in 2022, it combines sophisticated design by Jean-Michel Gathy with a serene atmosphere, creating an urban retreat in the bustling city.

Passalacqua (Lake Como, Italy)

Passalacqua is a luxurious and historic villa located on the shores of Lake Como, Italy, in the charming village of Moltrasio. Renowned for its opulence, privacy, and stunning views, it offers a quintessential Italian retreat. Originally built in the 18th century, Passalacqua has hosted notable figures such as Napoleon Bonaparte and composer Vincenzo Bellini.

Cheval Blanc (Paris, France)

Cheval Blanc Paris is an ultra-luxury hotel situated in the heart of Paris within the iconic La Samaritaine building, steps away from the Seine River and near major landmarks like the Louvre. It is managed by LVMH, blending haute couture elegance with impeccable service.

Ways You Can Travel

Car

Traveling by car for vacation offers unmatched flexibility, allowing you to explore destinations at your own pace, take scenic routes, and make spontaneous stops. It's cost-effective for groups, eliminates luggage restrictions, and turns the journey into part of the adventure. Plus, with the freedom to choose your stops, you'll discover hidden gems along the way.

Bus

Traveling by bus is affordable, eco-friendly, and stress-free. It lets you relax, enjoy the scenery, and avoid the hassles of driving or navigating. Many buses also offer comfortable seating and modern amenities.

Walking

Walking on vacation lets you discover hidden gems, explore at your own pace, and immerse yourself in local culture. It’s also a healthy and eco-friendly way to travel, minimizing your carbon footprint while enjoying your surroundings.

Top Destinations

Paris

Paris, the "City of Light," is famous for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre, charming streets lined with cafés, and a vibrant art and fashion scene. Whether you're exploring its historic neighborhoods or enjoying its exquisite cuisine, Paris offers an unforgettable cultural experience.

Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a captivating blend of ancient history, art, and vibrant street life. Visitors can marvel at iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and St. Peter’s Basilica, while enjoying world-class museums and delicious Italian cuisine. The city’s historic charm and bustling piazzas make it a top destination for travelers seeking culture, beauty, and timeless allure.

Amsterdam

Amsterdam is known for its charming canals, historic sites like the Anne Frank House, and vibrant culture. Its bike-friendly streets and lively neighborhoods make it an exciting destination for art, history, and exploration.
